An Introduction

“Life before death, strength before weakness, journey before destination.” - Brandon Sanderson

Pathfinder

I'm right there in the center of the above picture, under the well placed quote by Brandon Sanderson, aimed to show you how special I am. I enjoy candlelit dinners and long walks on the beach. When I saw long walks on the beach, I mean long. A lot of people give up within an hour and head back, I saw bring a tent.

Fun fact, if you copy and paste the above quote onto this library, you'll see how unique I am! I feel a lot of poeple like me have Imposter Syndrome but hopefully we all find a path worthy of us.

Wanderer

I spend most of my free time on two major activites. One is coding, I like to take on big projects and learn along the way, which expands my knowledge and the final product is usually applicable and usable! I have two favorite products I've had the pleasure of working on, one was the ERA application, which allowed Android users to get medical attention efficiently and I hope to expand it soon. The other is a music exploration web application, which I created after a short time working as a music curator. I took my knowledge of that and some machine learning (buzzword to get your attention, it was just a bunch of recommendation algorithms) to create a way for users to explore music in their own bubble. I hope to expand this and work on an exploration feature for movies, books, and more!

The rest of my time is spent reading books! Exciting isn't it? I've been reading ever since I could go into a library on my own, and I've been finishing them faster than Microsoft patches new releases. My favorite authors are Brandon Sanderson (who takes a break from writing by writing another book), Steven Erikson (the archaeologist gone author), and Terry Pratchett (who has gone for the long walk across the desert). A lot of other numerous authors could be mentioned here, but I'd be suprised if you got this far anyway.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me! It's useful to go out of this world and see it from the perspective of another one, so I hope to get to see your world soon.
